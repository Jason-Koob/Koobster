CompTIA

Recently I have been studying for the CompTIA Security+ Certification. I have been taking a course by Mike Chapple through LinkedIn Learning. The course goes into great detail for each topic coveredon the exam. I want to pass this certification so I have a firm foundation inside the computersecurity ecosystem.

This certification is part of CompTIA's stackable certification program. Firstly, I want to finish the Security+ certification because it will be the most beneficial for me as I prepare to take more security oriented courses. Their stacking certification system is unique as theyallow for people to take multiple different approaches to earning the same title.
